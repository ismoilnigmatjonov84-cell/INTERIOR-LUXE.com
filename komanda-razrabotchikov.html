Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add multiple rooms to a plan?

To add multiple rooms to your floor plan, switch to the "Draw Layout" mode. Start by drawing one room, then build on it to add the remaining rooms. You can manually enter the dimensions of each room to create an accurate and convenient layout for your space.

Where to start when creating an apartment remodeling project?

For a thorough design, create a plan in INTERIOR LUXE. Add all functional areas and display them in detail on the plan. To make your project more visual, visualize it in 3D using Interior Design 3D. The software not only allows you to create a realistic design but also calculate a renovation estimate, making the planning process even more convenient.

You're planning a renovation but lack design ideas. Where can you find inspiration?

If you're looking for interior design ideas, check out Houzz and Pinterest, where you'll find a wealth of stylish interior designs and room layout examples. You'll find ready-made ideas for every taste and style. And you can bring all your ideas to life and plan your layout down to the last detail using the online INTERIOR LUXE.

How to visually enlarge the space of a small apartment?

Use light colors for walls and furniture—pastel shades help create a sense of spaciousness. Place mirrors on the walls or in the decor: they reflect light and make the room feel airier. Try to minimize the number of large furniture pieces and choose multifunctional solutions, such as built-in wardrobes or convertible furniture. Also, avoid heavy curtains—opt for lightweight or translucent fabrics to let in more natural light.

How to create a project in Flat Planner

Step 1

Draw the walls

You can create rooms yourself using the "Draw Layout" mode: click where you want the corners to be. The service will automatically draw a rectangular room plan; just specify the length and width. For catalog templates, corners are moved manually.

Step 2

Add windows and doors

Start your free online apartment layout by placing windows and doors. Drag them to the desired wall while holding down the left mouse button. The grid will help you choose the exact placement. If you make a slight mistake, you can move a window or door to the side by dragging it.

Step 3

Arrange the furniture

Select a category and drag items into the scene directly from the catalog. There's no limit on the number of pieces of furniture. Click on an object and drag the square handles to resize and rotate it. You can specify the length and width in the bar at the top. Mirroring and reset buttons are also located there.

Step 4

Choose decor

Incorporate real-life elements into your 2D plan. Place toys next to a storage box, and place a houseplant on the dining table. Use larger plants for zoning: since they can be varied in size, you can account for their growth.

Step 5

Save the project

Download the result in PNG, SVG, or PDF format. Online interior design is free at this stage as well. There will be no watermarks in the document, but the wall lengths and room area will be preserved. The designer does not compromise quality; you can print the created plan in high resolution.
